EXAD is a native iOS marketplace specializing in exotic, adrenaline-fueled
adventures and extreme sports — the project where I went deep on building and
shipping a real, two-sided product on the App Store.

## The problem

Booking genuinely extreme experiences — think skydiving, supercar track days,
heli-adventures — is fragmented and trust-poor. EXAD set out to be a single,
curated marketplace where adventurers discover vetted experiences and providers
manage listings and bookings.

## What I built

I worked on the native iOS client in Swift and SwiftUI: the discovery and search
experience, rich experience-detail screens, and the booking flow, talking to a
REST backend with payments handled through Stripe.

## What I took away

EXAD taught me how a production iOS app actually ships — App Store review,
release management, payment integration, and the discipline of building a polished
two-sided marketplace where both buyers and providers depend on the same app.
