Nouri is a personalized nutrition coach that uses Whoop or Oura wearable data to
improve mental and physical health — better sleep, lower stress, more energy, or
sharper focus.

## The problem

Generic nutrition advice ignores how your body is actually doing today. Nouri
closes that loop: it reads your wearable metrics and long-term goals and turns
them into concrete, dietitian-backed actions.

## What I built

I contributed to the mobile app that delivers 6–8 daily food recommendations —
and an optional grocery cart — automatically tailored to each user's wearable
metrics. The work spanned onboarding, the daily recommendation feed, recipe
detail, and tracking surfaces, shipped to both the App Store and Google Play.

## What I took away

Nouri sharpened my skills at the intersection of mobile and health data: working
with wearable integrations, designing a daily-engagement loop, and shipping the
same product experience across iOS and Android.
