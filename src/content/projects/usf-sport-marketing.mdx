The USF Sport Marketing app was designed to enhance the visibility of the
University of San Francisco's Sport Marketing Association and improve its
communication channels with the student community.

## What I built

A native iOS app that surfaces the association's events, updates, and member
information — giving a student organization a real mobile presence and a direct
line to its target demographic.

## What I took away

This was an early shipped iOS app that connected my interest in sport with native
mobile development, and a lesson in building for a real community of users.
