Ripped Utopia is a fitness and powerlifting app built around a library of over
1,300 exercises and personalized workouts.

## The interesting part

The core engineering challenge was data: keeping a large exercise catalog and a
user's personal progress consistent between a local offline store and an online
database, so the app stays fully usable in the gym with spotty connectivity and
reconciles cleanly when it reconnects.

## What I built

Personalized workout generation, progress tracking, and the offline/online sync
layer — shipped to the App Store as a native iOS app.
